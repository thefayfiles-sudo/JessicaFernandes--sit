About Jess

I'm a Registered Kinesiologist with experience in injury rehabilitation and workplace functional assessments. As a flexologist, I provide assisted stretching and mobility programs to improve range of motion and reduce pain. I also work with individuals navigating disability claims to design practical return-to-work plans.

Outside of clinical work I'm an amateur barber with over 500 hours cutting men's hair — I offer tidy fades and classic cuts during select weekend hours.

Services & Programs

Rehab & Performance

Assessment, progressive exercise programs, pain management strategies, and home programs to speed recovery and improve function.

Workplace & Disability Case Management

Return-to-work plans, functional ability assessments, communication with employers and insurers, and supported transition programs.

Flexology — Assisted Stretching

Hands-on stretching sessions to improve mobility, reduce muscle tension, and complement strength training.

Barbering

Men's haircuts and fades; clean, tidy cuts for everyday styling. Mobile / pop-up appointments available by request.
